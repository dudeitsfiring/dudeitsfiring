Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Dude, It's Firing! ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Royal Fortune, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website at dudeitsfiring.com and our surf alert SMS service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what we need to provide surf alerts:

  • Name — to personalize your alerts
  • Phone number — to send you SMS surf alerts
  • Email address — if you choose email alerts instead of SMS
  • Selected surf spots — to know which spots to monitor for you
  • Subscription plan — to determine your spot limit

We do not collect mailing addresses, passwords, or payment card numbers. Payment processing is handled securely by Stripe — we never see or store your card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Send you surf condition alerts when your selected spots are firing
  • Send you a welcome message when you sign up
  • Process and manage your annual subscription via Stripe
  • Respond to support requests sent to privacy@dudeitsfiring.com

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing emails, or any purpose other than delivering the service you paid for.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

We share minimal information with trusted third parties only as needed to deliver the service:

  • Twilio — receives your phone number to deliver SMS alerts
  • Stripe — processes your payment. We share your name and email with Stripe for billing purposes. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • NOAA / National Weather Service — we query public buoy and weather data. No user data is shared.
  • OpenWeather — we query wind condition data. No user data is shared.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any other third parties.

4. SMS Communications

By signing up for SMS alerts, you consent to receive text messages from Dude, It's Firing! at the phone number you provide. Message frequency varies based on surf conditions — typically 0–4 messages per month per spot. Standard message and data rates may apply.

To opt out at any time, reply STOP or KOOK to any alert. You can also email us at privacy@dudeitsfiring.com to be removed. After opting out, you will receive one confirmation message and no further alerts.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your subscription is active. When you cancel or unsubscribe, we deactivate your account. You may request complete deletion of your data by emailing privacy@dudeitsfiring.com and we will remove your information within 30 days.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information. Your data is stored on secure servers. Payment information is never stored on our servers — it is handled entirely by Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure.

7. No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, advertising cookies, or any third-party tracking tools on our website. We do not track your browsing behavior.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@dudeitsfiring.com. We will respond within 45 days.

We do not sell personal information as defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify active subscribers of material changes via SMS or email. The updated policy will always be available at dudeitsfiring.com/privacy.

11. Contact Us
